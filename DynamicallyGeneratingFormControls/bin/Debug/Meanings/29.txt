Seven Of Cups 

[intepretation]
All that glitters are not gold and nothing is at it seems. Illusions play with his heart. 

[reversed]
She takes hold of her dreams and breaks free to make more of it. 

[symbols+images]
The merman faces cups, which play on his fantasies. Shadows and ghosts rising out of the cups, beckon him and draw him in all directions. They are merely illusions of mermaids leading him astray.

The reversed mermaid takes a hold of a "fantasy" makes it real by striving to achieve it.

Colour: colour of the oceans, orange, blues - associated with Pieces
